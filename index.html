Ancient wisdom answered for the modern seeker

Vedic Numerology is an ancient cosmic science rooted in both Chaldean and Pythagorean traditions. It studies the vibrational frequencies encoded in your birth date and name, revealing the celestial blueprint of your life path, personality, and destiny. Each number from 1–9 is governed by a specific planet, creating a unique stellar signature for every soul.

Every letter carries a specific Chaldean vibrational frequency. When combined, the letters of your birth name form a cosmic signal that broadcasts your soul's mission into the universe. Your name number (Expression Number) reveals your innate talents, deepest desires, and the energy you project into the world. That is why we ask for the full name given at birth.

Your Life Path Number is derived by reducing your full date of birth to a single digit (or a master number 11, 22, 33). It is the most significant number in your chart — the cosmic thread woven through every chapter of your life. It indicates your core purpose, the lessons you are destined to learn, and the gifts you carry into this incarnation.

Your phone number is not random — it emits a continuous vibrational frequency that interacts with your personal energy field daily. A number in harmony with your Life Path and Birth Number amplifies success, clarity, and positive connections. A discordant number can create subtle yet persistent energetic interference, draining your vitality and blocking opportunities.

In Vedic Numerology each digit is ruled by a celestial body: 1 → Sun ☉, 2 → Moon ☽, 3 → Jupiter ♃, 4 → Rahu (North Node), 5 → Mercury ☿, 6 → Venus ♀, 7 → Ketu (South Node), 8 → Saturn ♄, 9 → Mars ♂. Understanding which planets dominate your chart allows you to align daily actions with planetary energies for maximum cosmic support.

Celestial remedies are personalised prescriptions drawn from ancient Vedic texts — including specific mantras, gemstones, colours, rituals, and auspicious timings — designed to balance and strengthen weak planetary influences in your numerological chart. Thousands of seekers have reported tangible shifts in energy, focus, relationships, and fortune after consistently following their prescribed remedies.
